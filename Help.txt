KillEmALL!


Controls:
UpArrow - moving your ship to up;
DownArrow - moving your ship to down;
RightArrow - moving your ship to right;
LeftArrow - moving your ship to left;
Space - shot;
Esc - exit to menu;
Enter - select;


What you must did?
You must destroy all enemy ships and avoid asteroids.
You have 3 lives only.


GOOD LUCK, HAVE FUN!!!
